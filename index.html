About This Website

Project 4 is a simple website designed to help friends reconnect by sharing pictures and sparking meaningful conversations. Inspired by the challenges of staying in touch during the fast-paced college life, this project addresses the difficulty of maintaining connections with hometown friends as we not only grow apart geographically, but also emotionally. The website leverages the power of pathos, using nostalgic images to evoke memories of shared laughter, joy, and the bonds we’ve built over time. By providing an easy and heartfelt way to send a picture or a message, this platform encourages users to reach out, spark conversations, and remind each other of the enduring strength of their friendships—even when life gets busy.
